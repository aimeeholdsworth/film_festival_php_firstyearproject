About

Bristol Oscars Celebration is a one day film festival all about celebrating the newest and best films of the past year. Come and celebrate with us by watching Oscar winning films all day long! With a range of films from all of the different winners categories there is something for the whole family. So come and kick back and relax with us!

The film festival is set in the heart of Bristol, surrounded by the beauty and charm of the docks at the Watershed. The Watershed is a brilliant venue choice for our Oscars celebration as it has three state of the art cinema rooms, with plenty of comfy seating. Being on the docks means that you can stretch your legs between screenings whilst taking in some of the culture and beauty that Bristol has to offer. Enjoy a leisurly stroll in the sunshine (British weather permitting!) whilst admiring some of the historical artchitecture, or sit and chat at one of the many food and drink venues on the harbour side.
